Elevated Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice & lime juice
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice & orange juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon coconut extract & raspberry extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon blue food coloring (optional, for the signature color)

How to Make

  1. Prepare the Simple Syrup: In a saucepan, combine 1 cup of water and 1 cup of granulated sugar. Heat over medium heat, stirring until the sugar completely dissolves.
  2. Mix in the Juices: Add 1/2 cup of pineapple juice, 1/2 cup of lime juice, 1/4 cup of lemon juice, and 1/4 cup of orange juice to the simple syrup.
  3. Add the Extracts: Mix in 1/2 teaspoon of coconut extract and 1/2 teaspoon of raspberry extract. Add food coloring as needed for the color.
  4. Mix the Syrup in with club soda or add to a SodaStream and enjoy your homemade Baja Blast soda!
